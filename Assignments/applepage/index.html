Remembering Steve.


Over a million people from all over the world have shared their memories, thoughts and feelings about Steve. One thing they all have in common — from personal friends to colleagues to owners of Apple products — is how they’ve been touched by his passion and creativity. You can view some of these messages below. And share your own at rememberingsteve@apple.com

Honoring Steve

Mr. Jobs was an amazing inspiration to millions of people, including myself. As an artist I marveled at the beautiful care and craft that Steve demanded from all his products. His candor and precision spoke to all of us who aspire to follow our hearts and dreams and refuse to stop creating. While he will be sincerely missed, Steve will always be present in the global community he created and his numerous innovations that changed the way we all live. Thank you Steve for not just creating beautiful tools but for helping create a beautiful new generation.

Thomas

Insanely great

Cyd
